profiles

Profiles are systemwide settings that are applied by triggers. There are two categories, you can adjust:
  • services
    These settings can toggle some system services on and/or off.
    As said in battery drainers it's wise to disable syncing, wifi, etc. on some states.
    But switching services too often might as well cost more battery than it saves.
  • CPU settings
    By setting the virtual governor (or the CPU governor) its parameters and the cpu frequency.
    These settings determine the CPU's clockspeed ("how fast it is") and power consumption.
