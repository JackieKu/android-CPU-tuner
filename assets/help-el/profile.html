profiles

Profiles are systemwide settings that are applied by triggers. At the moment (v. 1.0) there's two categories, you can adjust:
  • CPU settings
    These settings determine the CPU's clockspeed ("how fast it is") and power consumption.
  • services
    These settings can toggle some system services on and/or off.
    As said in battery drainers it's wise to disable syncing, wifi, etc. on some states.
    You can leave [the service in question] unchanged, but you may as well enable or disable services with triggers, additionally you can go back to [the] previous state which activates the state the service was in before the last switch.
    If you manually override these settings, e.g. cause CPU tuner disables sync at 20% battery, but you need to sync you calendar with Google, this setting will stay untouched by CPU tuner unless a new trigger is activated and modifies the service's state.
