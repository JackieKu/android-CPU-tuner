battery drainers

There are no astral bugs/facehuggers leeching your battery empty! Here are the most common battery drainers listed:
  • display
    It's the number one drainer in any battery-powered system.
    The brighter, the worse.
  • mobile data
    Transmitting and receiving data wirelessly needs a huge amount of power, too.
    Due to design, 3G (UMTS, HSPA, etc.) drain the battery faster than 2G.
    Disabling data transfer in the background greatly helps improving the phone's runtime.
  • wifi
    Just as mobile data transfer, wifi virtually eats battery life.
    Having wifi enabled, your phone *always* scans for active wifi nearby. Go figure how good this is for your runtime.
    Better disable it completely, when you're not using it. There are good widgets for that task. ;)
  • bluetooth
    Take a look at "mobile data" and "wifi" and go figure..
  • the CPU itself
    Power usage of the CPU depends on the used governor and it's frequency
    To optimize this, you have just downloaded/installed CPU tuner. :)
